My Professional Development Plan

Read Layla's professional development plan and complete the practice activities to check your understanding and explore useful language for setting goals.

Model Text

For the coming year, I have identified a few key priorities for my professional growth.

Firstly, it will be essential to enhance my project management skills. I am currently taking an advanced online course so that I can lead our new community health project in Nablus more effectively.

Secondly, I plan to actively work on my public speaking. We often have to present our results to international donors, so I will need to communicate our impact with greater confidence and clarity.

Finally, I hope to improve my data analysis abilities. I must become more proficient with data visualization software in order to write more persuasive and evidence-based funding proposals. My long-term goal is to stay competitive in the job market and eventually move into a senior management role.
