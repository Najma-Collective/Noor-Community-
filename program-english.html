Our Vision

We believe language learning should feel joyful, relevant, and empowering. English Immersion is a space where multilingual identities shine and where learners gain the confidence to share their stories.

  • Conversation circles centered on storytelling, career goals, and civic engagement.
  • Digital literacy sessions that build research and presentation skills.
  • Collaborative projects showcasing participants’ cultures and aspirations.

Learning in Community

Neighborhood walks, museum visits, and service learning deepen language practice while strengthening community connections.

Program Snapshot

Our facilitators pair communicative language teaching with project-based learning. Participants explore local neighborhoods, interview community members, and co-create presentations that blend English practice with cultural exchange.

Morning & evening cohorts

Schedules adapt to work, childcare, and transportation needs.

Coaching pods

Learners receive weekly pronunciation and confidence coaching.

Peer leadership

Alumni mentors co-facilitate sessions and celebrate milestones.

Support Services

Transportation stipends, childcare partners, and multilingual counselors ensure learners can fully participate.

  • Meal stipends and technology lending libraries.
  • Wellbeing circles led by trained counselors.
  • Family workshops that extend learning at home.

Curriculum Highlights

Conversation Labs

Interactive dialogues, storytelling prompts, and civic engagement themes build vocabulary and fluency.

Experiential Learning

Field projects connect language practice to real-world contexts like community interviews and storytelling events.

Enrollment Journey

  1. Submit an interest form and meet with our placement team.
  2. Complete a friendly language assessment and orientation session.
  3. Join your cohort, meet your mentor, and co-create a learning plan.
