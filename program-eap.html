EAP Bridge

The English for Academic Purposes Bridge prepares refugee learners to enter college with a clear understanding of academic culture, expectations, and resources.

Sign up

Our Vision

Higher education should be accessible to every refugee learner. EAP Bridge demystifies campus systems and cultivates the academic literacy needed to succeed.

What We Do

Participants engage in integrated coursework that includes academic reading, lecture note-taking, presentation skills, and critical inquiry. We collaborate with partner colleges to align assignments and provide authentic practice.

Program Snapshot

  • Semester-long cohort with hybrid online and in-person sessions.
  • Workshops on academic integrity, research, and citation tools.
  • Campus visits and mentorship from current university students.

Curriculum Highlights

  • Critical reading labs exploring contemporary global issues.
  • Lecture simulations with peer feedback and professor guest speakers.
  • Capstone projects that map personal education plans and scholarship opportunities.

Support Services

Advising, technology access, and mental health referrals ensure learners are supported throughout their transition to college.
