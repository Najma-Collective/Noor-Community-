Mentoring Program

Our mentoring network pairs refugee learners with trusted guides who offer academic coaching, wellbeing support, and pathways into higher education and careers.

Become a mentee

Our Vision

Every learner deserves a champion. Our mentors affirm participant strengths, honor cultural identity, and co-create actionable plans for the future.

What We Do

We cultivate mentor-mentee matches based on interests, language background, and goals. Monthly gatherings bring the community together for skill-building, storytelling, and celebration.

Program Snapshot

  • Mentors include educators, university students, and industry professionals.
  • Goal-setting, financial literacy, and career exploration workshops.
  • Emergency microgrants and referrals to wraparound services.

Mentoring Framework

  • Strengths-based coaching that centers learner agency.
  • Peer circles for shared reflection and mental health check-ins.
  • Professional panels connecting mentees with internships and apprenticeships.

Support Services

Case management, translation support, and transportation stipends ensure mentees can engage in every opportunity.
