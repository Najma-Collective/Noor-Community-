Our Vision

Mentoring at Noor Community is relational, culturally responsive, and learner-led. We cultivate networks of support that honor lived experience and amplify learner agency.

  • Mentor onboarding rooted in trauma-informed and anti-racist practice.
  • Community circles that foster trust, healing, and shared leadership.
  • Career exposure that opens doors to internships, apprenticeships, and scholarships.

Mentors in Action

Professionals, alumni, and community elders co-facilitate mentorship circles, networking events, and leadership retreats.

Program Snapshot

Our mentoring model blends one-to-one coaching, peer cohorts, and family engagement to surround learners with consistent encouragement.

Mentor matching

Learners choose mentors based on shared goals, language, and interests.

Leadership labs

Workshops build public speaking, advocacy, and project management skills.

Family partnerships

Family nights strengthen communication and celebrate milestones.

Support Services

Mentors collaborate with staff to connect learners to scholarships, wellness care, and professional networks.

  • Career coaching with industry volunteers.
  • Wellbeing support through trauma-informed counselors.
  • Emergency funds for technology, transit, and essentials.

Mentoring Highlights

Career Pathways

Mentors host job shadowing days, career panels, and resume labs to expand professional horizons.

Wellbeing Circles

Gatherings center healing practices, reflection, and mutual aid that sustain learners throughout their journeys.

Enrollment Journey

  1. Submit a mentorship interest form and attend an orientation circle.
  2. Get matched with a mentor duo—one professional, one alumni peer.
  3. Co-design a growth plan and celebrate progress at quarterly gatherings.
