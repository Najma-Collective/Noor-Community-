Model Briefing: Jericho Youth Entrepreneurship Review

Adapted from Slide 15 of the main deck. Amani and Tareq prepare their evaluation pitch to the NGO leadership team in Ramallah.

Amani: We should acknowledge that the pilot appears to have generated strong participation numbers, even if the data set is relatively small.

Tareq: Agreed. We might emphasise that the mentorship component seems impactful, but we also need to flag the limited access to seed funding.

Amani: Right. We could argue that the model is scalable, provided that we secure municipal co-financing.

Tareq: Let's also note that some partners may have underestimated the training hours. We should propose a staggered schedule.

Amani: Good idea. I'll draft a recommendation that highlights these constraints while still backing the expansion.
