Writing Lab

Through collaborative workshops, one-to-one coaching, and publishing opportunities, the Writing Lab nurtures expressive, academic, and professional writing skills.

Request a consultation

Our Vision

Writing is a powerful tool for advocacy and healing. We invite learners to craft narratives, essays, and applications that reflect their resilience while meeting academic expectations.

What We Do

The Writing Lab integrates genre study, peer review, and multilingual resources. Facilitators scaffold every step of the writing process from brainstorming to final editing.

Program Snapshot

  • Weekly workshops focusing on narrative, analytical, and reflective writing.
  • Individual coaching for scholarship essays, resumes, and personal statements.
  • Publication opportunities through community zines and partner media outlets.

Curriculum Highlights

  • Story circles that center participant voice and heritage languages.
  • Research skill building with guided library and digital literacy sessions.
  • Editing intensives that teach collaborative revision and feedback strategies.

Support Services

We provide translation support, technology lending, and transportation stipends so writers can fully engage with the program.
