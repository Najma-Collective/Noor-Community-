Pre-Class Activity: Describing a Celebration

Complete the four tasks below before class. Use Ahmed's message about the festival to help you.

Learner instructions

Instructions for the learner: Welcome! This 30-minute activity will prepare you for our next class.

In class, we will talk about past celebrations. Please complete every activity before the lesson.

Model Text: A Message About a Festival

Read Ahmed's message to his friend Basem.

Hi Basem,

How are you?

Last weekend, I went to a food festival in Ramallah. It was a fantastic celebration! The festival was very interesting but also very crowded.

There was amazing live music on a stage. There was so much delicious food to try, like knafeh and shawarma. There were lots of people from different cities. There were many families with their children.

It was a really fun day.

Speak soon,
Ahmed

Activity 1. Check Your Understanding

Read the message again. Choose the correct answer for each question.

1. What is the message about?
2. How does Ahmed describe the festival?
3. What was NOT at the festival?

Activity 2. Vocabulary Match

Match the bold words from the message with their meanings.

1. celebration
2. interesting
3. crowded
4. fun

Activity 3. Grammar Sorting

Look at the nouns. Put them in the correct group based on the grammar from the message.

music
people
a stage
children
food
families

Activity 4. Complete the Sentences

Imagine you are at the same festival. Complete the sentences by choosing the correct option from the dropdown menu.
