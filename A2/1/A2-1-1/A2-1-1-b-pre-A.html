Warm-Up: How Was Your Weekend?

Prepare for the storytelling tasks in the Experiences & Storytelling lesson. Language here comes directly from the main slide deck A2/1/A2-1-1/A2-1-1-b.html. Follow the pathway to activate key past-tense phrases before class.

Rubric

Read the updated model dialogue carefully and answer each activity.

Model Dialogue: Monday Catch-Up

Adapted from Slide 7 of the main deck. Amal and Omar meet at the community workspace in Hebron on Monday morning.

Amal: Hey Omar! How was your weekend?

Omar: It was great. I went to Jericho on Friday to see my cousins.

Amal: Nice! What did you do there?

Omar: We took a walk through the old market, and we made short clips for our youth media project.

Amal: Sounds fun. I had a quiet weekend. I stayed in Hebron and joined the pottery workshop at the college. I finally made a cup!

Omar: Amazing! Did you share it on the project page?

Amal: Yes, I put the photos up on Saturday night.
