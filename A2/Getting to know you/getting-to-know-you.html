A Quiz About the Arab World

1. The ancient city of Petra is in which country?

A) Egypt
B) Jordan
C) Lebanon
D) Syria

Correct! Petra, known as the 'Rose City', is a famous historical and archaeological city in southern Jordan.

2. What is the capital of Morocco?

A) Casablanca
B) Marrakesh
C) Rabat
D) Fes

That's right! The capital city of Morocco is Rabat. While Casablanca is the largest city, Rabat is the capital.

3. The 'Cedars of God' is a famous forest in which country?

A) Lebanon
B) Syria
C) Jordan
D) Palestine

Yes! The Cedars of God (Horsh Arz el-Rab) is a protected forest and a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Bsharre, Lebanon.

4. Which sea is famous for being so salty you can float easily?

A) The Red Sea
B) The Mediterranean Sea
C) The Dead Sea
D) The Arabian Sea

Correct! The Dead Sea is a salt lake bordered by Jordan and Israel. Its high salt concentration makes the water very dense, allowing people to float effortlessly.

5. The world's tallest building, the Burj Khalifa, is in which city?

A) Doha
B) Dubai
C) Riyadh
D) Abu Dhabi

That's it! The Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world, is located in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

6. What is the traditional art of beautiful handwriting called?

A) Calligraphy
B) Mosaic
C) Engraving
D) Illumination

Perfect! Calligraphy is a visual art related to writing. It is the design and execution of lettering with a pen or other writing instrument. Arabic calligraphy is a major form of artistic expression in Islamic cultures.

7. Which country is the world's largest producer of dates?

A) Saudi Arabia
B) Egypt
C) Iraq
D) Iran

You got it! Egypt is the world's largest producer of dates, growing over 1.7 million metric tons annually.

8. The Rub' al Khali desert is also known as the...

A) Great Sand Sea
B) Empty Quarter
C) White Desert
D) Black Desert

Correct! The Rub' al Khali, which means "Empty Quarter" in Arabic, is the largest contiguous sand desert in the world.

9. What is the currency of the United Arab Emirates?

A) Riyal
B) Dinar
C) Dirham
D) Pound

Yes! The currency of the UAE is the Emirati Dirham, often abbreviated as AED.

10. In which country can you visit the great Pyramids of Giza?

A) Sudan
B) Egypt
C) Algeria
D) Libya

Exactly! The Great Pyramid of Giza and the other pyramids on the Giza plateau are located on the outskirts of Cairo, Egypt.
