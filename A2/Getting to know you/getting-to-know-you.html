Question 1

Which city is famous for the pyramids?

A) Beirut
B) Rabat
C) Giza
D) Doha

Question 2

Which drink is popular at celebrations?

A) Lemonade
B) Mint tea
C) Cola
D) Coffee latte

Sweet mint tea is a traditional drink for guests.

Question 3

Which country is Petra in?

A) Lebanon
B) Jordan
C) Oman
D) Algeria

Petra is a historic city carved into rock in Jordan.
