The Olive Harvest

Level: A2 Grammar: Past Simple vs. Past Continuous Duration: 60 Minutes

Part 1: Reading - Grandfather's Diary

Read this diary entry about the olive harvest in Salfit, 1998.

October 20, 1998 - Salfit

Today was the first day of the harvest. I woke up early. The sun was rising over the hills when we arrived at the grove. My sons, Mahmoud and Tariq, were preparing the nets under the trees.

While I was checking the branches, I saw that this year's crop was excellent. The olives were shining in the morning light. Mahmoud climbed the tallest tree while Tariq was spreading the cloth on the ground.

At noon, while we were resting under the shade, my wife brought us lunch. She made fresh bread and zaatar. We ate and talked about the old days. The children were playing nearby while we were eating.

By evening, we had collected twenty sacks. I felt proud. This land gave us everything.

Concept Checking Questions (CCQs)

Q1: "I woke up early." Is this a complete action or was it still happening?

Answer: Complete action

Q2: "The sun was rising." Was this finished or still in progress when they arrived?

Answer: Still in progress

Q3: Can we use Past Continuous for short, completed actions?

Answer: No, we use Past Simple

Part 5: Variant Text - News Snippet

Read this news report about olive farmers in Burin.

Local News - November 2, 2024

Burin farmers face challenges during harvest season

Yesterday morning, farmers from Burin village were trying to access their olive groves when they encountered blocked roads. "We were waiting for three hours," said Ahmad Najjar, a local farmer. "While we were standing there, we watched our trees from a distance."

Despite the difficulties, the community came together. Volunteers were helping families who couldn't reach their land. "When I heard about the situation, I immediately drove to Burin," explained Rania, a volunteer from Nablus. "While I was driving, I called other volunteers."

By sunset, they had managed to collect ten sacks from accessible areas. The community spirit was strong.
