About Noor Community

We are an educator-led collective supporting refugee learners through trauma-informed instruction, peer mentorship, and community-building initiatives.

Our Vision

Every learner deserves to feel seen, encouraged, and equipped to pursue their aspirations. We cultivate learning environments where multilingual identities are celebrated and where students can reimagine their futures with confidence.

Our Story

Noor Community grew from volunteer-led study circles into a coordinated network of teachers, counselors, and student leaders. Over the years we have expanded to serve learners across multiple cities while retaining a focus on relationship-centered support.

Key milestones include:

  • Launching our English Immersion program to meet urgent language needs for newly arrived youth.
  • Creating the Writing Lab to demystify university expectations and elevate personal narratives.
  • Building the EAP Bridge to connect learners to higher education pathways and scholarships.
  • Expanding mentorship to include alumni coaches, career panels, and wellbeing resources.

How We Work

Our curriculum design and learner support approach center three pillars: collaborative pedagogy, culturally sustaining practice, and holistic wellbeing. We design experiences that welcome family voices, leverage community expertise, and reduce barriers to participation.

Core Commitments

  • Language justice through translanguaging and accessible resources.
  • Academic readiness through scaffolded projects and feedback loops.
  • Career exploration via mentoring, internships, and storytelling.
