Activity 2

Speed greeting in breakout rooms

Pair up and ask your partner a few of these questions. You will switch partners after a few minutes.

  • What is your favourite film or show?
  • Which superpower would you like to have?
  • If you could time-travel, where would you go?
  • Share one fun fact about yourself.
  • What is a quote or saying you love?
Game time

Arab World Pub Quiz

Work together to choose the best answer. After each question we will reveal the correct choice.

Question 1

Where is the ancient city of Petra?

A) Egypt
B) Jordan
C) Lebanon
D) Syria

Petra, the "Rose City", is a historic site in southern Jordan.

Question 2

What is the capital of Morocco?

A) Casablanca
B) Marrakesh
C) Rabat
D) Fes

While Casablanca is the largest city, Rabat is the capital.

Question 3

Which sea is famous for letting you float easily?

A) Red Sea
B) Mediterranean Sea
C) Dead Sea
D) Arabian Sea

The Dead Sea's high salt concentration keeps swimmers afloat.

Question 4

Where can you visit the Burj Khalifa?

A) Doha
B) Dubai
C) Riyadh
D) Abu Dhabi

Dubai in the UAE is home to the world's tallest building.

Question 5

The "Cedars of God" forest is in which country?

A) Lebanon
B) Syria
C) Jordan
D) Palestine

The forest is a UNESCO World Heritage Site in northern Lebanon.

Question 6

What is the art of beautiful writing called?

A) Calligraphy
B) Mosaic
C) Engraving
D) Illumination

Arabic calligraphy is a celebrated art form across the region.

Question 7

In which country can you visit the pyramids of Giza?

A) Sudan
B) Egypt
C) Algeria
D) Libya

Egypt's Giza plateau is home to the Great Pyramid and Sphinx.

Question 8

Which spice blend is famously used in Levantine cooking?

A) Garam masala
B) Za'atar
C) Ras el hanout
D) Herbes de Provence

Za'atar is a savoury mix of thyme, sesame, and sumac.

Question 9

Which city is nicknamed the "Pearl of the Desert"?

A) Marrakech
B) Muscat
C) Riyadh
D) Tunis

Marrakech is known for its vibrant markets and desert setting.
