Let's Play a Game!

A Quiz About the Arab World

1. The ancient city of Petra is in which country?

A) Egypt
B) Jordan
C) Lebanon
D) Syria

Correct! Petra, known as the 'Rose City', is a famous historical and archaeological city in southern Jordan.

2. What is the capital of Morocco?

A) Casablanca
B) Marrakesh
C) Rabat
D) Fes

That's right! While Casablanca is the largest city, Rabat is the capital of Morocco.

3. Which sea is famous for being so salty you can float easily?

A) The Red Sea
B) The Mediterranean Sea
C) The Dead Sea
D) The Arabian Sea

Correct! The Dead Sea is a salt lake bordered by Jordan and Israel. Its high salt concentration makes the water very dense.

4. The world's tallest building, the Burj Khalifa, is in which city?

A) Doha
B) Dubai
C) Riyadh
D) Abu Dhabi

That's it! The Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world since 2009, is located in Dubai, UAE.

5. The 'Cedars of God' is a famous, ancient forest in which country?

A) Lebanon
B) Syria
C) Jordan
D) Palestine

Yes! The Cedars of God is a protected forest and a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Bsharre, Lebanon.

6. What is the traditional art of beautiful handwriting called in the Arab world?

A) Calligraphy
B) Mosaic
C) Engraving
D) Illumination

Perfect! Arabic calligraphy is a major form of artistic expression in Islamic cultures.

7. In which country can you visit the great Pyramids of Giza?

A) Sudan
B) Egypt
C) Algeria
D) Libya

Exactly! The Great Pyramid of Giza is located on the outskirts of Cairo, Egypt.

8. The Rub' al Khali desert is also known as the...

A) Great Sand Sea
B) Empty Quarter
C) White Desert
D) Black Desert

Correct! The Rub' al Khali, which means "Empty Quarter" in Arabic, is the largest contiguous sand desert in the world.

9. What is the currency of the United Arab Emirates?

A) Riyal
B) Dinar
C) Dirham
D) Pound

Yes! The currency of the UAE is the Emirati Dirham, often abbreviated as AED.
