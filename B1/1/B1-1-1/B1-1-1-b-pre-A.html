Model Dialogue: Coffee Break Connections

Based on Slide 9 of the main deck. Rasha from Ramallah and David from Dublin meet between conference sessions.

Rasha: Hi, I'm Rasha. I coordinate partnerships for BuildBright Labs in Ramallah.

David: Nice to meet you, Rasha. I'm David, a product owner with Emerald Innovations. I lead the content teams.

Rasha: Great! I usually connect startups with mentors and track our partnership agreements.

David: That aligns with our work. I plan sprint goals and make sure every designer meets the deadlines.

Rasha: Do you collaborate with the learning team in Ramallah?

David: Yes, I do. I share weekly reports so both teams stay aligned.
