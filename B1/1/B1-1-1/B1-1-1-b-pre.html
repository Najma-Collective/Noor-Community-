Pre-Class Worksheet: Ramallah-Dublin Tech Summit Networking

Welcome! Complete this pathway before class to get ready for the Global Connect: Ramallah-Dublin Tech Summit networking simulation. Work through each task in order to understand the model dialogue, review professional roles, and practise Present Simple responsibilities.

Rubric

Read the model dialogue carefully and answer each activity based on it.

Model Dialogue: Meeting at the Global Connect Summit

Read the conversation between Hanan from Ramallah and Patrick from Dublin during a speed networking round.

Hanan: Hi, I'm Hanan. I'm a software developer at Super-Coder in Ramallah. My team builds language-learning apps for schools.

Patrick: Great to meet you, Hanan. I'm Patrick, a project manager with Emerald Innovations in Dublin. I coordinate our new partnerships in the MENA region.

Hanan: That sounds exciting. I usually collaborate with our UX designer, Lina. She designs every screen and checks user feedback.

Patrick: We could learn from that. Our marketing specialist runs the launch campaigns, but we still need stronger learning content.

Hanan: I also work with our data analyst. She tracks how many learners finish each module, so I can improve the features.

Patrick: Maybe we can connect her with my team. We monitor results, and I prepare weekly reports for the directors.

Hanan: Do you manage the launch schedule too?

Patrick: Yes, I plan the timeline and make sure each team meets their deadlines. Let's exchange emails after this round.
