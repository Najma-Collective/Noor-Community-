Today's Aims

By the end of this lesson, you will be able to...

  • Use common greetings and introductions.
  • State your name and professional role.
  • Use sentence stress to highlight key information.
  • Introduce yourself confidently to a new colleague at a company event.

Main Task

Task

Task Preparation

Prepare for the Networking Event

You are a new employee at Future Forward Tech, a leading technology company in Ramallah.

Today is the company-wide welcome event for all new hires. Your goal is to network and meet your new colleagues.

You see someone you don't know. Go and introduce yourself.

Task

Role-Play: Company Welcome Event

You will be moved into breakout rooms. Use the role cards to have a conversation. Find out your partner's name and role. Remember to use the correct greetings, introductions, and sentence stress.

Role Card A

Role: New Software Developer

Task: It's your first day. Introduce yourself (name and role), and find out your colleague's name and role. Say, "Nice to meet you."

Role Card B

Role: New Marketing Specialist

Task: It's your first day. Respond with your name and role, and say, "Nice to meet you, too."

Reporting

Debrief with Your Team Lead

Sara, your team lead, couldn't attend the event. She asks: "So, who did you meet?"

Take turns introducing the colleague you met in the breakout room.

Sentence frame: "I met [Partner's Name]. They are a new [Partner's Role]."
