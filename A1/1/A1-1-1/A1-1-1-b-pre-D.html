Warm-Up: Introductions

This worksheet prepares you for the Introductions | Meeting New People lesson. All language is drawn from the main slide deck A1/1/A1-1-1/A1-1-b.html. Complete each activity in order to learn the key phrases you will use in class.

Rubric

Read the model dialogue carefully and answer each activity.

Model Dialogue: First Meeting at the Hub

Maha and Sami meet new volunteers at the Noor Community orientation in Ramallah.

Maha: Hi, I'm Maha. I'm from Ramallah, and I'm a community organiser with Noor Community.

Sami: Nice to meet you, Maha. I'm Sami. I'm from Bethlehem, and I'm a civil engineering student.

Maha: This is Lina. She's our new volunteer. She's a graphic designer.

Lina: Hello! I'm excited to join the orientation today.

Sami: Welcome, Lina. Are you joining the youth innovation project?

Lina: Yes, I am. I'm ready to help with the digital posters.
