Warm-Up: Meeting New People

This worksheet prepares you for our lesson on introductions. Complete the activities to learn key phrases for class. All language is from the main slide deck A1/1/A1-1-1/A1-1-b.html.

Model Dialogue: First Meeting

Maha and Sami meet a new volunteer, Lina, at the Noor Community orientation in Ramallah.

Maha: Hi, I'm Maha. I'm from Ramallah, and I'm a community organiser.

Sami: Nice to meet you, Maha. I'm Sami. I'm from Bethlehem. I'm a civil engineering student.

Maha: This is Lina. She's our new volunteer. She's a graphic designer.

Lina: Hello! I'm excited to join the orientation.
