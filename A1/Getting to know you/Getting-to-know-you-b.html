Task Preparation

Your Question Menu

Question Type 1

  • Do you like cooking?
  • Do you like reading?
  • Do you like walking?

Question Type 2

  • How often do you study English?
  • How often do you watch TV?
  • How often do you drink coffee?

Choose the question that fits your partner. Listen carefully and note their answer.

Main Task

Let's Interview!

Your Goal

  • Ask 3 classmates a question.
  • Learn one new fact about each person.

How to Play

  • The teacher will choose you to start.
  • Pick a classmate and choose a question.
  • Listen and note their answer.
  • Say “Thank you!” and switch roles.

Example

  • You: “Fatima, how often do you read?”
  • Fatima: “I sometimes read in the evening.”
Reporting Time
