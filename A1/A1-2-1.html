Pre-Class Activity: Joining the Palestinian Museum

Complete the five tasks below before class. Use what you learned about joining the Palestinian Museum to answer each question.

Learner instructions

Instructions for the Learner: Welcome! This activity will prepare you for our next class.

Please complete all the activities below. It will take about 30 minutes. See you in class!

Model Dialogue: Getting a Membership Card

Listen to the audio of the conversation and read the text below.

Receptionist (Tariq): Hello. Welcome to the Palestinian Museum. How can I help you?

Visitor (Fatima): Hello. I'd like a membership card, please.

Tariq: Of course. I just need some details. What's your name?

Fatima: My name is Fatima Khalil.

Tariq: And where are you from, Fatima?

Fatima: I am from Palestine.

Tariq: Great. And what's your phone number?

Fatima: My phone number is 059-123-4567.

Tariq: Perfect. And your email address?

Fatima: It’s fatima.k@email.com.

Tariq: Thank you. Here is your card.

Activity 1. Check Your Understanding

Read the dialogue and choose the correct answer for each question.

1. Where is Fatima?
2. What does Fatima want?

Activity 2. Vocabulary: Questions and Details

Match the receptionist's questions to the personal details Fatima gives.

1. What's your name?
2. Where are you from?
3. What's your phone number?
4. What's your email address?

Activity 3. Grammar Focus: Build the Questions

Click and drop the words into the correct order to make the questions from the dialogue.

1. Question about name

2. Question about nationality

3. Question about phone number

Activity 4. Practice: Complete the Conversation

Complete the sentences in the dialogue by choosing the correct option from the dropdown menu.

Read the dialogue again and complete the membership form with Fatima's information.
