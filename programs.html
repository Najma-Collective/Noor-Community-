Programs that Nurture Confidence and Belonging

Our learning pathways combine language development, academic readiness, and wraparound mentoring so refugee students can thrive in their studies and communities.

Our Vision

We create welcoming classrooms where refugee learners explore new ideas, practice emerging skills, and gain the courage to lead. Each program is designed to lower barriers, foster connection, and amplify learner voice.

What We Do

Noor Community programs integrate trauma-informed facilitation, localized resources, and community partnerships. Our teams co-design curriculum with learners, ensuring every session is responsive to their goals and lived experiences.

Program Pillars

  • Language fluency for academic and everyday contexts.
  • Critical thinking and research skills for higher education.
  • Holistic mentoring that addresses wellbeing and belonging.
